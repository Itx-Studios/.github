
# Itx-Studios

---

## Who We Are

Itx-Studios is a team of developers, designers, and enthusiasts dedicated to pushing boundaries in:

- **Machine Learning** - from research experiments to production-ready models, we work on projects involving computer vision, natural language processing, reinforcement learning, and more.
- **Game Development** - building immersive, fun, and engaging games across platforms using modern engines and tools.
- **Software Projects** - creating utility apps, experimental tools, back-end systems, and full stack solutions that solve real problems or explore new ideas.

---

## What We Do

- Build open source projects you can learn from & contribute to  
- Experiment with cutting-edge technologies and frameworks  
- Collaborate across disciplines: AI, UX, graphics, systems, and more  
- Share knowledge: code, documentation, blog posts, demos, etc.  

---

## Get Involved

We welcome contributions in many forms:

- **Code contributions** - bug fixes, new features, optimization 
- **Testing & feedback** - help with QA, UX testing, or documentation  
- **Ideas & research** - proposals for new projects, experiments, algorithmic improvements
